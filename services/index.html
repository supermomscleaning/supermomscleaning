Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. Does Supermom bring her own cleaning supplies?
  2. Yes! Supermom provides her own cleaning supplies for any type of cleaning necessary. If you have a preference of brand or type of supplies, let her know before your scheduled cleaning.
  3. What does "COVID-19 certified" mean?
  4. Supermom takes the pandemic seriously and will do everything she can to keep herself and her clients safe. Currently, she serves 13 clients on a regular or semi-regular basis. Supermom will wear a mask to all first-meetings and interviews, as well as going the extra mile to keep surfaces clean and disinfected.
  5. What if I'm not satisifed with Supermom's cleaning job?
  6. Let us know as soon as possible! Supermom guarantees customer satisfaction and will return to your residence to do another cleaning until it meets your standards.
  7. I think the price of (insert service) is too high. Can I get a discount?
  8. Feel free to ask! Rates are based on competitive analyses of cleaning businesses in the area, travel requirements, size of the residence, and what services you would like done.
  9. Do you accept (insert name of credit card) here?
  10. If it's accepted by Square, we can accept it! Supermom now uses the Square app for all service payments, or you can pay in cash or with Venmo/Zelle/etc.
  11. My residence is (insert number of miles) away. Would Supermom be willing to come out this far?
  12. Supermom has a general distance limit of no more than 50 miles from the greater southern VT area. That said, feel free to inquire! Note that we may charge more for cleaning jobs that are located further away to cover the cost of gas and travel time.
